Pro Tips

What makes a strong resume

  • Use strong action verbs — Engineered, Optimized, Launched, Drove results
  • Always quantify achievements — "Improved speed by 40%", "Managed $2M budget"
  • Include relevant keywords from job descriptions to pass ATS scanners
  • Keep it to 400–800 words — concise and impactful
  • List specific tools and technologies — not just "familiar with databases"
  • Use consistent formatting — avoid tables, columns, graphics for ATS
  • Tailor your resume for each application by mirroring the job description
